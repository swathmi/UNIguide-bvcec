Frequently Asked Questions

What is UNIGuide?

UNIGuide is an AI-powered smart assistant designed specifically for BVCEC students. It provides instant access to academic information, student services, course details, placement resources, and campus-related queries through an intelligent chatbot interface available 24/7.

Is UNIGuide available 24/7?

Yes, the UNIGuide chatbot is available 24/7 to assist you with your queries. However, official support team responses to feedback and detailed requests are typically provided during business hours (Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST).

Can UNIGuide answer department-specific questions?

Absolutely! UNIGuide is equipped to handle department-specific queries for all 9 departments at BVCEC (CSE, AIML, CSM, CAD, CIVIL, ECE, EEE, IT, MECH). You can ask about curriculum, faculty, placements, and department-specific resources.

How do I use the AI Chatbot?

Simply navigate to the AI Chatbot section from the homepage and start typing your questions. The intelligent system will understand your query and provide relevant information. No special formatting or commands are needed—just ask naturally!

Is my data secure with UNIGuide?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All interactions are encrypted and your personal information is protected according to institutional guidelines. We do not share your data with third parties.

How can I provide feedback or report an issue?

You can use the feedback form below to share your suggestions, report bugs, or provide feedback. You can also contact our support team via email at support@bvcec.edu.in with detailed information about your issue.
