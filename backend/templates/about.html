About UNIGuide

Your AI-powered smart campus assistant helping BVCEC students access information instantly and efficiently

What We Do

🎯 Our Mission

To transform the student experience at BVCEC by providing instant, accurate, and accessible information through intelligent AI assistance, eliminating information silos and enhancing academic and campus engagement.

🚀 Our Vision

To become the go-to digital assistant for every student at BVCEC, enabling seamless access to academic resources, career development tools, and campus services in one unified platform.

💡 Why UNIGuide?

Students spend valuable time searching for information scattered across multiple resources. UNIGuide consolidates college information and provides intelligent, instant answers through 24/7 AI-powered support.
