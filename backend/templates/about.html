About UNIGuide

Your AI-powered smart campus assistant

What is UNIGuide?

UNIGuide is an AI-powered campus assistant designed to help BVCEC students quickly access college-related information such as academics, student services, events, and important documents.

Why UNIGuide?

Students often face difficulty finding accurate information. UNIGuide simplifies this by providing instant responses through an intelligent chatbot interface available 24/7.
